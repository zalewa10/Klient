What We Do?

At NAMETT5, we validate the feasibility of LLM-driven use cases. Explore how we can assist you in achieving the desired outcomes.

Prototyping LLM-Powered Services
By collaborating closely with your team, we will rapidly develop initial versions of services. This step emphasizes integrating LLM capabilities into user interfaces, aiming to create a design that is both intuitive and engaging. To assist in generating effective ideas and swiftly transforming them into business value, we will concentrate on iterative development and user feedback in subsequent steps. This approach accelerates the journey from concept to market-ready solutions.
Rethink, Measure, Iterate
This step focuses on analyzing usage patterns and refining the service architecture. It involves consistently adjusting and examining user engagement with LLM-powered services. The goal is to identify areas for improvement, enhance efficiency, and optimize the overall user experience. This is achieved by making adjustments based on real-world usage data and feedback from selected users in your team.
Evaluating in Production
In this step, we deploy the LLM services on our servers for final end-to-end testing. The focus is on making key recommendations for optimization, considering factors like performance, throughput, and cost. We also evaluate the trade-offs involved in these optimizations, ensuring that the deployment is robust and efficient and providing reproducibility of LLM pipelines.
Production-Ready Stack
The final step involves specifying a hardware stack infrastructure that is tailored for inference in your specific use case. Additionally, we provide a software stack that facilitates installation and manages upgrades. This step is crucial for making data-driven decisions for implementation, ensuring that the service is not only technically sound but also aligns with your business objectives and operational requirements.
